软件系统分析与设计作业2

简答题

用简短的语言给出对分析，设计的理解

  • analysis emphasized an investigation of the problem and requirements, rather than a solution.
  • Design emphasizes a conceptual solution that fulfills the requirements, rather than its implementation.

用一句话描述面向对象的分析与设计的优势

  • the analysts don’t have to be “language experts”
    • the experts in the problem domain and the implementation-level experts can communicate using a common notation.

简述UML的作用。考试考哪些图

  • the function is to visualize the results of analysis and design.

从软件本质的角度，解释软件范围(需求)控制的可行性

  • 由于软件本身的复杂性，不可见性，不一致性，可变性，软件范围多数情况下对于客户和开发者都是模糊的。
